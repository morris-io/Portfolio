FantasyFairway.com

A fantasy golf web app allowing users to form leagues, draft players and to compete between eachother.

  • Every Tuesday a new PGA tournament and its participating golfers load in. Turn by turn, each user drafts 4 golfers to their team. Every golfer and their score are then displayed and updated underneath of their team's name on the leaderboard page.
  • Connected to ESPN and SportContentApis golf leaderboard for official scores and participents, eliminating the need to manually update any tournament data.
  • Built with: React, Node.js. Presented on: MongoDB, Vercel, Render, GitHub.
  • Live site (optimized for mobile): fantasyfairway.com
